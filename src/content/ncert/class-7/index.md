**Class 7 History (Our Pasts-2)** is among the most UPSC-relevant NCERTs. It covers medieval India in depth — the Delhi Sultanate, the Mughal Empire, Bhakti and Sufi movements, tribal communities, and regional cultures — themes that form the backbone of GS1 medieval history questions. Class 7 Geography introduces the environment, inside the Earth, atmosphere, water cycle, and climate — foundational for GS3 environment questions.

**Class 7 Science** covers nutrition, respiration, transportation in organisms, forests, and waste management — directly tested in GS3 environment and science.

> **Note on rationalized chapters:** NCERT removed several chapters in 2022–23. This site covers **all original chapters** including removed ones, as UPSC draws from the full original corpus.
