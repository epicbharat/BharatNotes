Class 9 Geography covers India's physical geography — location, relief, drainage systems, climate (monsoon), vegetation, and wildlife. Essential for UPSC GS1 Geography.

**Contemporary India — I** covers six chapters:

- **Chapter 1:** India — Size and Location
- **Chapter 2:** Physical Features of India
- **Chapter 3:** Drainage
- **Chapter 4:** Climate
- **Chapter 5:** Natural Vegetation and Wildlife
- **Chapter 6:** Population

For UPSC aspirants, this book forms the bedrock of GS1 Indian Geography. Questions on rivers, mountains, climate, and biodiversity frequently appear in both Prelims and Mains. Every chapter here should be studied alongside maps.
