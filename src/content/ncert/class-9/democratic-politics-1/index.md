Class 9 Political Science introduces democracy, constitutional design (South Africa, India), electoral politics, Parliament, and fundamental rights. Foundation for UPSC GS2.

**Democratic Politics — I** covers five core chapters:

- **Chapter 1:** What is Democracy? Why Democracy?
- **Chapter 2:** Constitutional Design
- **Chapter 3:** Electoral Politics
- **Chapter 4:** Working of Institutions
- **Chapter 5:** Democratic Rights

For UPSC aspirants, this book is foundational for GS2 (Indian Polity and Governance). The concepts of democracy, constitutional design, electoral politics, and fundamental rights directly feed into UPSC Mains answers on Indian democracy, civil liberties, and institutional functioning.
