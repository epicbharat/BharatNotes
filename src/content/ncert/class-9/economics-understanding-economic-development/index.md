Class 9 Economics introduces fundamental concepts through India-specific case studies — a model village (Palampur), human resource development, poverty, and food security. Foundation for UPSC GS3.

**Understanding Economic Development** covers four chapters:

- **Chapter 1:** The Story of Village Palampur
- **Chapter 2:** People as Resource
- **Chapter 3:** Poverty as a Challenge
- **Chapter 4:** Food Security in India

For UPSC aspirants, this book is foundational for GS3 (Indian Economy). The concepts of factors of production, disguised unemployment, poverty measurement, food security, and the role of the Public Distribution System are all directly relevant to UPSC Mains.
