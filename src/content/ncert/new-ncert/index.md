## About the New NCERT Curriculum

NCERT began releasing completely redesigned textbooks from **2024–25** onwards, starting with Class VI. These are not merely rationalized versions of old books — they are new books with new frameworks, updated content, and an integrated approach:

- **Class VI (2024–25):** Two new books released — *Curiosity* (Science) and *Exploring Society: India and Beyond* (integrated Social Science — History + Geography + Civics combined into one book)
- **Class VII (2025–26):** *Exploring Society: India and Beyond* Part I & Part II released
- **Class VIII (2026–27):** New books in progress

These new books are relevant for UPSC because:
1. UPSC questions may reference concepts from the new books as they enter mainstream education
2. The new integrated Social Science approach introduces contemporary themes (environment, globalisation, local governance) in new ways
3. The updated science content reflects current knowledge and policy priorities
