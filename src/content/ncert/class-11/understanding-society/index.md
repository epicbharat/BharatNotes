**Understanding Society** (Class XI Part 2) moves from concepts to structural analysis — how societies are stratified, how caste operates as a social institution, what gender means beyond biology, how tribe relates to the nation-state, and how environment and society interact. It also introduces key western social thinkers. All chapters are directly relevant to UPSC GS Paper 1 Indian Society section.
