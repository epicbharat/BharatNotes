**Themes in World History** (Class XI) traces the broad arc of human civilisation — from early humans and pastoral societies to empires, the Renaissance, industrialisation, colonialism, and the 20th century world. For UPSC, this book is foundational for GS Paper 1 (World History section) and supplements answers on colonialism, nationalism, and the post-war order.
