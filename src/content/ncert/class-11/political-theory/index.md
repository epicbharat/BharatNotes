**Political Theory** (Class XI) is a foundational NCERT for UPSC aspirants. It introduces the normative concepts that underpin every GS2 (Governance and Polity) and GS4 (Ethics) question: What is freedom? What constitutes justice? What are rights? How does citizenship work? Who is a nationalist? What is secularism? These chapters provide the conceptual vocabulary for writing substantive Mains answers.

Unlike descriptive NCERTs that catalogue facts, this book asks you to *reason* about political concepts — precisely the skill UPSC Mains rewards. A candidate who has internalised this book can write analytically about reservation policy, sedition law, uniform civil code, nuclear deterrence, or development-versus-environment tradeoffs. Each chapter maps directly to recurring Mains themes.
