**Introducing Sociology** (Class XI) lays the conceptual foundation for understanding Indian society. It introduces what sociology is, how social institutions (family, marriage, kinship, religion, economy) work, what culture means, how socialisation shapes individuals, and how inequality and exclusion operate. These concepts form the analytical vocabulary for UPSC GS Paper 1 Indian Society and GS Paper 2 Social Justice answers.
