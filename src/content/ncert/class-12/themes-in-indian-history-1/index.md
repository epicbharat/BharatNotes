**Themes in Indian History Part I** (Class XII) covers ancient and early medieval India through a thematic lens — from Harappan civilisation to the Mauryan Empire, early cities, social histories, the Bhakti-Sufi traditions, the Vijayanagara Empire, and Mughal agrarian society. These themes are directly tested in UPSC GS Paper 1 (Ancient and Medieval India).
