**Themes in Indian History Part I** (Class XII) covers ancient India through a thematic lens — from Harappan civilisation to the Mauryan Empire, early cities, social histories, and agrarian societies. These themes are directly tested in UPSC GS Paper 1 (Ancient and Medieval India).
