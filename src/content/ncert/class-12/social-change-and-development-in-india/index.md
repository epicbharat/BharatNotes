**Social Change and Development in India** (Class XII Sociology) examines how Indian society has changed since colonial times and continues to change today. It covers structural change, the environment-society nexus, rural-agrarian transformation, industrialisation, urbanisation, tribals and forests, caste transformation, women's movements, and religious communalism. Every chapter directly feeds UPSC GS Paper 1 (Indian Society section) and GS Paper 2 (Social Justice section).
