**Introductory Macroeconomics** (Class XII) covers national income accounting, money and banking, income determination, government budget and the economy, and open economy macroeconomics. Provides the conceptual foundation for UPSC GS Paper 3 questions on GDP, fiscal deficits, monetary policy, and balance of payments.
