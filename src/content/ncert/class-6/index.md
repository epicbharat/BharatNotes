**Class 6 Social Science** builds the foundational knowledge that UPSC GS1 stands on. The History book covers India's earliest civilisations (Harappan), Vedic society, Janapadas, Mahajanapadas, the Mauryan Empire, post-Mauryan developments, and the Gupta period — the exact chronology tested in Prelims. Geography covers Earth's physical structure and India's physical features. Civics introduces democracy, governance, and social equality — foundational for GS2.

**Class 6 Science** covers living organisms, food and nutrition, materials, motion, and electricity — concepts that appear in UPSC GS3 environment and science-technology questions.

> **Note on rationalized chapters:** NCERT removed several chapters in 2022–23. This site covers **all original chapters** including removed ones, as UPSC draws from the full original corpus. Removed chapters are marked *(Rationalized — removed in 2022)*.
