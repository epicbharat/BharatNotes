Class VIII Civics is the most important civics NCERT before Class 11. It covers the Indian Constitution and its key features, secularism, how Parliament works, how laws are made, the criminal justice system, marginalisation of Dalits and Adivasis, public facilities as rights, and law as an instrument of social justice. These chapters map directly to GS2 governance and polity questions.
