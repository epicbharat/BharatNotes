## Overview

Post-Independence India (1947 onwards) encompasses the monumental task of nation-building — integrating 565 princely states, establishing democratic institutions, pursuing economic development through planned industrialisation, defending sovereignty in wars, and navigating the Cold War through non-alignment. The period also saw critical challenges: the Emergency, linguistic reorganisation, and the 1991 economic crisis that transformed India's trajectory.
