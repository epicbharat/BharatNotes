## Overview

International Relations is a **core GS2 section** in UPSC, covering India's foreign policy evolution, bilateral relationships with neighbours and major powers, the UN system and international organisations, global trade architecture, and multilateral forums. This section provides twenty-nine comprehensive chapters that combine factual depth for Prelims with analytical frameworks for Mains — from Panchsheel and NAM to the Quad, BRICS expansion, and India's WTO battles.
